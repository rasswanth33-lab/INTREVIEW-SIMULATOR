# 🌐 Campus Connect

An inter-college platform connecting students to collaborate on projects, discover opportunities, and build teams.

---

## 🚀 Overview

**Campus Connect** is a platform designed to bridge the gap between students in inter college.
It enables students to connect, form teams, collaborate on projects, and explore opportunities such as hackathons, internships, and technical communities.

The goal is to create a unified ecosystem where students can grow together beyond campus boundaries.

---

## ✨ Features (Planned)

- 👥 Student Profiles
- 🔎 Discover students across colleges
- 🤝 Team formation for projects & hackathons
- 📢 Opportunity sharing (events, internships, competitions)
- 💬 Collaboration & communication tools
- 🔐 Secure authentication system
